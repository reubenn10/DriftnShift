Guidelines for Drivers


1. Drive safe and sober.
2. Respect traffic laws and fellow drivers.
3. If applicable, wear glasses or contacts when driving.
4. Document any driving-related expenses, like fuels and tolls.
5. Check the car regularly to ensure gas, tire pressure and all car fluids are at appropriate levels.
6. Report any damages or problems with their assigned cars to HR as soon as possible.
7. Avoid double-parking, blocking entrances and engaging in other traffic violations that may result in fines.
8. If you are fatigued or sick you should avoid driving if you feel your driving ability is impaired.
